My Top 10 Favorite Songs

Top 1:

It's Called: Freefall by Rainbow Kitten Surprise

The song speaks about the burden of living. It reveals the thought process of a person too tired to keep going.

Top 2:

All I want by Kodaline

It is a song about loss and the desire to fill in the space left by another.

Top 3:

Heaven Up There by Palace

A song that talks about the uncertainty of what lies after death. Doubt and the feeling of being lost are core themes in the song.

Top 4:

No Surprises by Radiohead

Is living a quiet, boring life and going by each day with the same monotonous routine a good way to spend your time here on earth? The song tackles this question.

Top 5:

Ode To The Mets by The Strokes

For me, this piece is a tribute to the past. A song full of nostalgia and painful memories.

Top 6:

You'll Be Safe Here by Rico Blanco

The song reminds me of my friends back in Senior High School. Listening to this makes me feel as if I'm back with them.

Top 7:

Become The Warm Jets by Current Joys

A song about being consumed by depression and wanting to go back to the past.

Top 8:

I'll Try Anything Once by The Strokes

This song holds many meanings for me. It is a confusing yet enjoyable listen.

Top 9:

From Eden by Hozier

It talks about the beauty and tragedy present in a person.

Top 10:

Lady Lie by Rainbow Kitten Surprise

It is the self-destructive pursuit of someone or something that causes nothing but harm.
